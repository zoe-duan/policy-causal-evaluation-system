# Robustness Checklist

- Alternative pre/post windows.
- Alternative comparison groups or donor pools.
- Placebo treatment dates.
- Placebo outcomes that should not move.
- Negative-control units.
- Clustered/robust standard errors.
- Heterogeneous effects pre-specified or held out.
- Excluding units with ambiguous treatment.
- Spillover buffer zones.
- Multiple testing correction or transparent family of outcomes.
- Recompute after removing influential units.
