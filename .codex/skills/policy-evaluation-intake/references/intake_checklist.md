# Policy Evaluation Intake Questionnaire

1. What policy/event changed?
2. Where and when did it change?
3. Who was treated? Who could be a comparison group?
4. What outcome should change, through what mechanism, and over what time window?
5. What is the desired estimand: ATT, ATE, CATE, LATE, dynamic effect, or policy value?
6. What data exist at unit × time level?
7. Are there pre-treatment periods?
8. Are there likely anticipation effects?
9. Are there spillovers between treated and control units?
10. What other policies or shocks happened at the same time?
11. What would convince a skeptical reviewer?
12. What would make the design invalid?
