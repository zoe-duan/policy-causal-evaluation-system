# Policy extraction checklist

- Is the source official or only a lead?
- Are publication, effective, and implementation dates separated?
- Is the treated unit list explicit?
- Are exemptions and intensity rules extractable?
- Does implementation vary by jurisdiction or phase?
- Are there enforcement records or budget flows that can proxy actual exposure?
- Can the policy be mapped to the outcome data unit and time frequency?
- Does any concurrent policy threaten identification?
